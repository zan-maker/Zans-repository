## Target Profiles

### Ideal Fractional CFO Prospects
- **Stage:** Seed to Series C
- **Revenue:** $1M-$20M ARR
- **Employees:** 10-100
- **Signals:** Recent funding, hiring spree, operational complexity
- **Pain Points:** Cash flow management, investor reporting, financial modeling, fundraising prep

### Other Opportunities
- Interim CFO placements
- Financial due diligence for investors
- M&A advisory support
- Financial systems implementation

## Output Format
Each lead must include:
1. Company name and profile
2. Trigger event (why now?)
3. Decision maker identification
4. Pain point hypothesis
5. Recommended approach
6. Value proposition tailored to them
7. Outreach draft (if requested)
8. Priority score (1-10)
