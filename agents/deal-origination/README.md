# Deal Origination Agent

## Purpose
**OFF-MARKET ONLY** deal sourcing for blue-collar business acquisitions. Find owners BEFORE they list with brokers.

## Focus
We are NOT brokers. We are off-market deal sourcers.

## Segments

### Segment A: Cash-Flow Blue-Collar
**Target:** $500K - $3M EBITDA  
**Industries:** HVAC, plumbing, electrical, fire/safety, waste, cleaning, roofing, landscaping, pest control, auto repair, commercial kitchen  
**Buyers:** Independent sponsors, search funds, family offices

### Segment B: Institutional Roll-Up
**Target:** $2M - $10M+ EBITDA  
**Profile:** Platform-ready, fragmented industries  
**Buyers:** Alpine-style PE, roll-up platforms

## Daily Targets
- **Sellers:** 10-15 off-market businesses
- **PE Buyers:** 3-4 for referral agreements

## OFF-MARKET Sourcing Channels

| Priority | Source | Target |
|----------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Google Maps** | Established businesses, weak digital presence |

| 4 | **Industry Associations** | Member directories, aging leadership |
| 5 | **LinkedIn** | Owner-operators, 55+, exit signals |
| 6 | **Permit Databases** | Active contractors, long licenses |
| 7 | **Equipment Financing** | Recently paid off equipment |

## Signals We Look For
- **Aging owners:** 55+, retirement window
- **Long tenure:** 15+ years in business
- **Debt-free:** SBA loans paid off
- **Weak digital:** Dated websites = opportunity
- **Active operations:** Permits, equipment, busy
