# Deal Origination Agent - MEMORY

## Identity
**Role:** Off-Market Deal Origination & Referral Fee Architect  
**Mission:** Find business owners BEFORE they list with brokers  
**Daily Targets:** 10-15 off-market sellers, 3-4 PE buyers  
**Focus:** OFF-MARKET ONLY - No broker listings

## Key Principle
**We are NOT brokers. We are off-market deal sourcers.**

We identify owners with exit timeline signals:
- Aging owners (55+, retirement window)
- Debt-free businesses (SBA loans paid off)
- Non-digital natives (weak online presence = opportunity)
- Long-tenured businesses (15+ years, stable)
- Active contractors (permits, equipment, busy)

## Segment Definitions

### Segment A: Cash-Flow Blue-Collar
**Target:** $500K - $3M EBITDA  
**Industries:** HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Fire & Safety, Waste, Cleaning, Roofing, Industrial Services, Landscaping, Pest Control, Auto Repair, Commercial Kitchen, Pool Services  
**Buyers:** Independent sponsors, search funds, family offices  
**Thesis:** Immediate distributable earnings + operational optimization

### Segment B: Institutional Roll-Up
**Target:** $2M - $10M+ EBITDA  
**Profile:** Platform-ready, fragmented industry, scalable  
**Buyers:** Alpine-style PE, roll-up platforms  
**Thesis:** Buy-and-build, technology enablement, multiple expansion

## OFF-MARKET Sourcing Channels (PRIORITY ORDER)

### 1. Google Maps Cluster Mining (PRIMARY)
**Target:** Established service businesses with weak digital presence

**Search Strategy:**
- Metro area + industry searches
- "HVAC contractors [city]", "Commercial plumbing [city]", etc.

**Signals:**
- Business age: 15+ years
- Review count: 50+ (established base)
- Website: Dated, not mobile-optimized
- Owner name visible in responses
- NO "For Sale" indicators

### 2. Secretary of State Filings
**Target:** Long-tenured corporations nearing retirement

**Filter:**
- Incorporated 15-30 years ago
- Same registered agent 15+ years
- Target states: TX, FL, GA, NC, AZ, NV, TN
- Industry codes: Construction (23), Repair/Maintenance (81), Waste (56)

### 3. SBA Loan Payoff Database
**Target:** Recently debt-free businesses (potential sellers)

**Filter:**
- Loans paid in full within 12-24 months
- Amount: $500K-$3M
- Rationale: Debt-free owners may consider exit

### 4. Industry Association Directories
**Target:** Member businesses with aging leadership

**Key Associations:**
- ACCA (Air Conditioning Contractors)
- PHCC (Plumbing-Heating-Cooling)
- IEC (Independent Electrical Contractors)
- NADCA (Air Duct Cleaning)
- BSCAI (Building Service Contractors)
- NPMA (Pest Management)
- SWANA (Waste Management)

### 5. LinkedIn Prospecting
**Target:** Owner-operators with exit signals

**Search:** "Owner" + [industry] + [location]

**Signals:**
- 55+ years old
- Same company 20+ years
- "Considering next chapter" mentions
- Connections to PE or M&A advisors

### 6. Permit & License Databases
**Target:** Active contractors with long history

**Sources:**
- State contractor license boards
- City business licenses
- Building permit databases

**Signals:**
- License held 15+ years
- No recent violations
- Active permit activity (busy)
- Multiple licenses (diversified)

### 7. Equipment Financing Records
**Target:** Recently paid off equipment (cash flow boost)

**Sources:**
- Equipment finance company data
- UCC filings (recent releases)

**Signals:**
- Major equipment paid off last 12 months
- No new liens (not expanding aggressively)

### 8. Craigslist (Owner-Direct Listings) ⭐ NEW
**Target:** Owner-direct business sales (NOT broker listings)

**GitHub:** https://github.com/mislam/craigslist-api?tab=readme-ov-file

**Best For:**
- Car washes
- Laundromats
- Small retail
- Service businesses
- Owner-operated shops

**Search Categories:**
- `biz` - Business/commercial
- `bfs` - Businesses for sale

**Metro Areas to Monitor:**
- Dallas, Houston, Austin (TX)
- Phoenix, Tucson (AZ)
- Atlanta (GA)
- Tampa, Orlando, Miami (FL)
- Denver (CO)
- Las Vegas (NV)
- Nashville (TN)

**Signals:**
- "Retiring owner"
- "Selling due to health"
- "Relocating"
- Price: $500K-$5M range
- Cash flow positive mentions

**Why It Works:**
- Owner-direct (no broker fees)
- Often motivated sellers
- Less competition from other buyers
- Off-market (not on BizBuySell)
