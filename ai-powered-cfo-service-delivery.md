# AI-Powered Fractional CFO Service Delivery

**Strategic Priority:** PRIMARY revenue driver  
**Target:** 3-5 clients at $5K-$15K/month = $15K-$75K/month  
**Timeline:** First client in 30-60 days  
**Method:** AI agents deliver work product, human (Sam) provides oversight & client relationships

---

## The Model: Human + AI Agents

### Sam's Role (Human-in-the-Loop)
- **Client relationships** - Meetings, calls, trust building
- **Strategic decisions** - Major recommendations, board presentations
- **Quality assurance** - Review AI outputs before delivery
- **Final approval** - Client-facing deliverables
- **Pricing & contracts** - Fee structure, engagement terms

### AI Agents' Role (Service Delivery)
- **Financial modeling** - 3-statement models, forecasts, scenarios
- **KPI dashboards** - Real-time metrics, automated reporting
- **Variance analysis** - Budget vs. actual, trend identification
- **Cash flow management** - 13-week forecasts, working capital optimization
- **Investor reporting** - Board decks, investor updates
- **Process automation** - Month-end close, reconciliation
- **Research** - Industry benchmarks, competitive analysis

## Quality Assurance Framework

### Sam's Review Checklist
Before any client deliverable:
- [ ] Numbers reconcile to source data
- [ ] Logic makes sense (sanity check)
- [ ] Client context applied (not generic)
- [ ] Strategic insight added (not just data)
- [ ] Format appropriate for audience
- [ ] No errors or typos
- [ ] Consistent with previous periods

### AI Agent Quality Gates
- **Reporter:** Auto-check formatting, completeness
- **Forecaster:** Backtesting vs. actuals
- **Reconciler:** Exception flagging
- **Watchdog:** Anomaly detection
- **Advisor:** Confidence scoring (1-10)

### Client Feedback Loop
- Monthly: Client satisfaction score
- Quarterly: Service review
- Annually: Strategic planning
- Continuous: Iterate based on feedback

### Client Churn

- **Mitigation:** Sam reviews everything

- **Mitigation:** Train AI agents continuously
- **Process:** Document all client preferences
- **Backup:** Standard operating procedures

### Scaling Bottlenecks
- **Mitigation:** Productized services
- **Process:** Templates, automation
