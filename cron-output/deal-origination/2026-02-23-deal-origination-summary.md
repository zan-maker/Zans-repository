## Executive Summary

The blue-collar M&A market remains exceptionally active, driven by PE roll-up strategies in HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and waste management. The "Silver Tsunami" of baby boomer retirements is creating significant deal flow, with 2.3-3 million SMBs expected to change hands over the next decade. Competition is intensifying—PE interest in skilled trades jumped from 8% to 23% of new buyers (2023-2024).

## Seller Sourcing: Off-Market Opportunities

### Key Indicators for Off-Market Potential

1. **Age Profile**
   - Businesses owned by individuals 60+ years old
   - Founders approaching retirement age (65-75)

2. **Digital Footprint Signals**
   - Outdated websites (last updated 5-10+ years)
   - Minimal/no online presence
   - No active social media or marketing

3. **Business Characteristics**
   - Established 20-50+ years
   - Healthy financials but stagnant growth
   - Owner-operator dependent (no clear succession plan)
   - Family-owned with no interested heirs

4. **Operational Signals**
   - Still using legacy systems/processes
   - No modern CRM or business management tools
   - High customer concentration (repeatability indicates value)

### Target Industry Sweet Spots

| Industry | Typical Revenue | PE Interest | Off-Market Signals |
