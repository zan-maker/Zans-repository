## Seller Sourcing Strategies

### Off-Market Opportunity Identification

**Demographic Triggers:**
1. **Baby Boomer Retirement Wave**
   - Succession planning in high gear (2026 peak year)
   - Many owners lack family successors
   - ESOPs and management buyouts being evaluated

2. **Family Business Transitions**
   - Second/third generation owners seeking liquidity
   - Lack of interest from children in trades businesses
   - Financial complexity of family business sales

3. **Owner Burnout**
   - Skilled trades labor shortage
   - Difficulty hiring and retaining technicians
   - Regulatory compliance burden increasing

**Operational Triggers:**
1. **Revenue Plateau**
   - Single-operator businesses hitting growth ceiling
   - Need for professional management systems
   - Desire for operational infrastructure from PE backing

2. **Succession Urgency**
   - Health issues or unexpected events
   - Desire to "cash out" while business is healthy
   - No qualified internal successor

3. **Market Pressure**
   - Consolidation driving competitive pressure
   - PE-backed competitors investing in marketing and technology
   - Difficulty competing on price and service

## Valuation & Deal Structure Trends

### Multiples (2025-2026 Benchmarks)

- **HVAC Residential Service:** 4.5x - 7.0x EBITDA
- **HVAC Commercial:** 5.0x - 7.5x EBITDA
- **Plumbing Services:** 4.0x - 6.5x EBITDA
- **Electrical Services:** 4.5x - 7.0x EBITDA
- **Multi-Trade Platform:** 5.5x - 8.0x EBITDA

### Premium Valuation Drivers

1. **Recurring Service Revenue**
   - Service contracts (subscription model)
   - Maintenance agreements
   - Annual recurring revenue (ARR) > 40% of total

2. **Management Depth**
   - Second-tier leadership team
   - Systems and processes documented
   - Owner semi-absentee or willing to stay

3. **Geographic Density**
   - Concentrated service area (reduces travel costs)
   - Adjacent territory expansion potential
   - Cross-selling opportunities

4. **Technology Investment**
   - CRM and dispatch software
   - Marketing automation
   - Financial reporting systems

### Common Deal Structures

- **Platform Acquisition:** 60-80% equity to PE, 20-40% rollover
- **Add-On Acquisition:** 70-90% equity to platform, 10-30% rollover
- **Seller Financing:** Typically 10-20% with 3-5 year term
- **Earn-Out:** 10-25% of total purchase price (performance-based)
- **Management Incentive Plans:** 5-10% equity pool for key employees

**Bear Case Factors:**
