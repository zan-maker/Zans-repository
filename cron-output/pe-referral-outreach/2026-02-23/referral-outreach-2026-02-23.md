## Referral Agreement Terms (Standard)

**Fee Structure:**
- Referral fee: 5% of transaction value up to $1M
- Sliding scale above $1M (typically 3-4% for larger deals)
- Minimum fee: $50,000

**Payment Terms:**
- Trigger: Closing (includes deferred + assumed debt)
- Payment timing: Net 30 days
- Attribution: 12-month tail from introduction
- Exclusivity: Non-exclusive (I can work with multiple buyers)

**What PE Firms Get:**
- First look at off-market opportunities
- Direct owner contact (no intermediaries)
- Pre-qualified sellers (motivated, realistic expectations)
- Detailed business summaries before introduction

**What I Need from PE Firms:**
- Investment criteria (industries, size range, geography)
- Ideal deal profile
- Decision timeline
- Preferred communication method
