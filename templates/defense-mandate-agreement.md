### 2.2 Exclusivity

☐ **Exclusive Mandate:** Client will not engage any other financial advisor for the Transaction without Advisor's prior written consent.

☐ **Non-Exclusive Mandate:** Client may engage additional advisors with written notice to Advisor.

### 2.3 Tail Period

(a) The counterparty was introduced to Client by Advisor during the term; or

## 3. COMPENSATION

### 3.1 Monthly Retainer
Client shall pay Advisor a monthly retainer of [$25,000/$35,000/$50,000] (the "Retainer"), payable in advance on the first business day of each month.

(a) The Retainer shall be payable for the duration of the engagement, up to a maximum of [6/12] months.
(b) The Retainer is non-refundable.
(c) [50%/100%] of Retainer payments shall be credited against the Success Fee.

### 3.2 Success Fee
Upon closing of a Transaction, Client shall pay Advisor a success fee (the "Success Fee") calculated as follows:

| Transaction Value | Success Fee % |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Up to $50 million | 5.0% |
| $50M - $100M | 4.0% |
| $100M - $250M | 3.0% |
| $250M - $500M | 2.5% |
| Above $500M | 2.0% |

(a) **Minimum Fee:** In no event shall the Success Fee be less than [$500,000/$750,000/$1,000,000].

(b) **Fee Calculation Example:**
   - Transaction Value: $150 million
   - Fee: $150M × 3.0% = $4,500,000
   - Less: Retainer credits (if applicable)
   - Net Success Fee: $4,500,000

### 3.3 Break Fee
If Client enters into a definitive agreement for a Transaction and such Transaction is not consummated due to Client's breach or failure to perform, Client shall pay Advisor a break fee equal to [50%/100%] of the estimated Success Fee.

### 3.4 Expense Reimbursement
Client shall reimburse Advisor for all reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the engagement, including:

(a) Travel and accommodation (economy/business class for flights over 4 hours)
(b) Legal and professional fees (with prior approval)
(c) Data room and technology costs
(d) Printing, marketing, and presentation materials
(e) Due diligence expenses

## 4. DEFINITIONS

### 4.1 "Transaction"
"Transaction" means any of the following involving Client or its subsidiaries:

(a) Sale of all or substantially all of the assets;
(b) Sale of a majority of the outstanding equity;
(c) Merger, consolidation, or business combination;
(d) Recapitalization or restructuring;
(e) Minority investment exceeding [20%] of equity;
(f) Joint venture or strategic partnership involving [material] assets;
(g) Any other transaction resulting in a change of control.

### 4.2 "Transaction Value"
"Transaction Value" means the aggregate value of consideration paid or payable, including:

(a) Cash paid at closing;
(b) Fair market value of any securities issued;
(c) Assumed indebtedness;
(d) Earn-outs and contingent payments (valued at maximum);
(e) Value of retained assets or non-compete arrangements.

## 6. TERMINATION

### 6.1 Termination for Convenience
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon [30/60/90] days' written notice to the other Party.

### 6.2 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:

(a) The other Party materially breaches this Agreement;
(b) The other Party becomes insolvent or bankrupt;
(c) The other Party fails to cure a breach within [10/15/30] days of notice.

### 6.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:

(a) Client shall pay all accrued fees and expenses;
(b) The Tail Period (Section 2.3) shall apply;
(c) Confidentiality obligations shall survive;
(d) Advisor shall return Client materials within 30 days.
