## 4. TERM & TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of **3 months** (the "Initial Term").

### 4.2 Renewal
After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews on a **month-to-month basis** unless terminated by either party.

### 4.3 Termination
- **By Client:** Client may terminate with **30 days written notice** after Initial Term
- **By CFO:** CFO may terminate with **60 days written notice** to allow for transition
- **For Cause:** Either party may terminate immediately for material breach

### 4.4 Transition Upon Termination
- CFO will provide reasonable transition support for up to 30 days
- All work product and deliverables remain Client's property
- Access to systems will be revoked per agreed timeline

## 6. DATA & SECURITY

### 6.1 Data Access
Client grants CFO access to:
- Accounting systems (QuickBooks, Netsuite, etc.)
- Bank accounts (read-only via Plaid or similar)
- Payroll systems
- Other financial data as needed

### 6.2 Data Security
CFO agrees to:
- Maintain SOC 2 Type II equivalent security standards
- Encrypt data in transit and at rest (AES-256)
- Use OAuth 2.0 for API connections
- Store credentials securely (password manager)
- Limit access to authorized personnel only
- Maintain daily backups

### 6.3 Confidentiality
All Client data remains strictly confidential. CFO will not disclose Client information to third parties without written consent.

### 6.4 Data Ownership
All financial data, reports, and deliverables created remain Client's property. CFO retains the right to use anonymized, aggregated data for industry benchmarking.

### 6.5 Data Return/Deletion
Upon termination, CFO will:
- Return all Client data within 30 days
- Delete all copies from systems within 60 days
- Provide certification of deletion if requested

## 7. AI TOOLS & AUTOMATION

### 7.1 AI Agent Usage
CFO uses AI-powered tools to enhance service delivery, including but not limited to:
- Automated data processing
- Financial forecasting models
- Report generation
- Anomaly detection

### 7.2 Human Oversight
All AI-generated outputs are reviewed by experienced CFO personnel before delivery to Client. Strategic decisions and recommendations always involve human judgment.

### 7.3 AI Training
Client data is NOT used to train AI models. All processing is done in isolated environments with no data retention by AI vendors.

### 7.4 Service Levels
- AI tools supplement, not replace, CFO expertise
- Client receives same quality as traditional fractional CFO
- Response times may be faster due to automation
- Complex issues always involve human consultation

### 8.2 Client Responsibilities

### 8.3 Limitations
CFO services do NOT include:
- Tax preparation or filing (refer to CPA)
